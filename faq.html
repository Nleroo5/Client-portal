Sales Dashboard FAQ

Everything you need to know about your sales performance dashboard

Point System

How does the point system work?

The point system rewards both activity and results to encourage consistent effort while prioritizing actual conversions:

Activity Points (Low Value):
  • 0.1 point per call made
  • 0.1 point per email sent
Engagement Points (Medium Value):
  • 3 points per discovery call scheduled
  • 5 points per discovery call completed
Conversion Points (High Value):
  • 10 points per deal moved to onboarding
  • 20 points per deal gone live

Your total weekly score is calculated automatically and displayed on the Team Leaderboard.

Why are some activities worth more points than others?

The point values reflect business impact:

  • Low-value activities (calls, emails) are important for volume but don't guarantee results
  • Medium-value activities (discovery calls) show engagement and progress
  • High-value activities (onboarding, live deals) represent actual revenue generation

This balanced approach ensures reps are rewarded for both effort and outcomes.

How do I log activities to earn points?

There are two ways to log activities:

  1. Quick Log Buttons (This Week tab): Click buttons like "Call Made" or "Discovery Scheduled" to instantly log activity
  2. Deal Card Actions (My Deals tab): Click "Call" or "Email" buttons on individual deal cards

Both methods automatically update your scorecard and team leaderboard in real-time.

Sales Pipeline

What are the stages in the sales pipeline?

Your pipeline has 8 stages that represent the customer journey:

  1. Qualified - Lead has been vetted and meets criteria
  2. Cold Outreach - Initial contact attempts
  3. Follow-Up - Continuing communication with interested prospects
  4. Discovery Scheduled - First meeting booked
  5. Discovery Completed - Initial consultation finished
  6. Onboarding Portal - Deal accepted, client entering onboarding
  7. Campaign Live - Active paying customer
  8. Follow-Up & Resell - Maintaining relationship for upsells
How do I move deals between stages?

You have three options:

  • Stage Dropdown: Click the dropdown on any deal card and select a new stage
  • Next Button: Click the "Next" button to move the deal to the next sequential stage
  • Edit Deal: Click the edit button (✏️) and change the stage in the modal

When you move a deal, its "last updated" timestamp refreshes automatically.

What does "stalling" mean?

A deal is marked as stalling when it has been in the same stage for more than 7 days without any updates.

Stalling deals appear:

  • With a "STALLING" badge on the deal card
  • In the "Stuck Deals" section on the Action Items tab
  • In the "Today's Focus" zone on My Deals tab

Take action on stalling deals by logging a call, sending an email, or moving them to the next stage.

Can I edit or delete deals?

Yes! Each deal card has two buttons in the top-right corner:

  • Edit (✏️): Opens the deal editor to update company name, contact info, email, phone, or stage
  • Archive (🗑️): Moves the deal to "closed-lost" status, removing it from your active pipeline

Archived deals are not deleted permanently - they're stored with a "closed-lost" stage.

Weekly Targets & Metrics

How do I set my weekly targets?

On the This Week tab, click the "Edit My Targets" button to set your personal weekly goals for:

  • Calls Made
  • Emails Sent
  • Discovery Calls Scheduled
  • Discovery Calls Completed
  • Deals to Onboarding
  • Deals Gone Live

Your targets are personal and can be adjusted anytime based on your capacity and goals.

When does the week reset?

The sales week runs from Monday to Sunday. Your scorecard resets every Monday at midnight, giving you a fresh start each week.

Historical data is preserved, so you can always review previous weeks' performance.

What happens if I exceed my targets?

When you hit or exceed a target, you'll see:

  • A "Target hit!" message on that metric
  • The progress bar shows 100% completion
  • Your points continue to accumulate beyond the target

There's no limit to how many points you can earn - targets are goals, not caps.

Dashboard Features

What is "Today's Focus"?

Today's Focus is your personalized priority zone on the My Deals tab. It highlights:

  • Stalled Deals: Deals that haven't been updated in over 7 days
  • Discovery Calls Today: Deals in "Discovery Scheduled" stage
  • Quick Wins: Deals in onboarding or live stages that need attention

This helps you prioritize your daily activities for maximum impact.

How does the Team Leaderboard work?

The Team Leaderboard (Team tab) ranks all sales reps by their weekly point total.

  • Updated in real-time as activities are logged
  • Shows rank, name, and total points
  • Your personal rank is displayed at the top
  • Top 3 performers are highlighted with #1, #2, #3 badges

Use this to track your performance against teammates and stay competitive.

What are Action Items?

The Action Items tab shows tasks that need your attention, organized by urgency:

  • Stuck Deals: Deals stalling for more than 7 days
  • Overdue: Tasks past their due date
  • Due Today: Tasks due today
  • Upcoming: Future tasks on your calendar

You can mark tasks as done or snooze them to a later date.

How do I add a new deal?

Click the "+ Add New Deal" button on the My Deals tab. You'll be prompted to enter:

  • Company Name (required)
  • Contact Name
  • Email
  • Phone
  • Pipeline Stage (required)

The deal will appear immediately in your pipeline under the selected stage.

Technical & Account

Is my data saved automatically?

Yes! All actions are saved to the cloud immediately:

  • Logging activities
  • Moving deals between stages
  • Editing deals or targets
  • Completing tasks

You can safely close your browser or switch devices - your data is always synced.

Can I access the dashboard on mobile?

Yes! The dashboard is fully responsive and works on all devices:

  • Mobile phones (iOS and Android)
  • Tablets
  • Desktop computers

The layout automatically adjusts for optimal viewing on your screen size.

Who can see my deals and activity?

Privacy controls:

  • Your Deals: Only visible to you and admins
  • Your Scorecard: Visible to all team members (for leaderboard)
  • Your Targets: Personal and private
  • Your Tasks: Only visible to you and admins

Team members can see your point total but not individual deal details.

How do I log out?

Click the "Logout" button in the top-right corner of the dashboard header. You'll be redirected to the login page.

For security, always log out when using shared devices.
